Internship Opportunity

We are a cleaning services company with an extensive client base in the London district and beyond. We provide a very serene working environment for all our colleagues and make sure to present safe conditions, tools and services for both clients and workers in any place we deliver our services.


Intern's Job Responsibilities

  • Understands the overall concept of the company, including the brand, customer satisfaction, goals, and all other aspects of the services we render.
  • Rotates through our divisions of responsibility and provides ideas to grow and improve the business.
  • Accepts designated, client-focused services, propose ideas and solutions, and delivers the best services possible to the client.
  • Engages with clients and provides service and/or sales of cleaning solutions.
  • Provide suggestions to management for improving customer service and internal processes.
  • Learns and becomes acquainted with the cleaning tools and detergents used for best services.

Work Hours & Benefits

We provide a considerate working hours that can be discussed further with all possible interns who are willing to work us. We also provide a good compensation for you services rendered during the internship service with us.

Have an aptitude for work

Intern Qualifications / Skills

  • Great Verbal communication.
  • Great Organizational skills
  • Timely and aware of Schedule
  • Professionalism and Customer focus
  • Confidentiality

Education, Experience, and Licensing Requirements

  • 1st to 4th year of college graduate program
  • Prior industry experience is preferred
