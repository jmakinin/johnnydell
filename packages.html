How it works

You have a package you need delivered to Ghana, give us a ring or leave us a message. We will arrange with you to have it delivered to our location in London, UK. From whichever location or country you may be in.

If you are close to our location, we can come pick it up or you can drop it off at our office personally and we will take the right details from you and start the shipment process to any location in Ghana of your choosing.
